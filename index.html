About Dior

Dior is a world-renowned luxury fashion house founded in 1946 by Christian Dior. Known for its elegance and innovation

The brand has set the standard in haute couture, ready-to-wear, accessories, and fragrances, becoming a global symbol of sophistication and style.

How we began

Christian Dior launched his fashion house in Paris in 1946, introducing the groundbreaking "New Look" in 1947.

This revolutionary design, with its emphasis on femininity and bold silhouettes, redefined post-war fashion and cemented Dior's place as a leading name in luxury fashion.
